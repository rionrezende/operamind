# OperaMind — AI Assessment Call Script (45 Minutes)

## Pre-Call Preparation (5 min before)
- Review their scanner results (industry, size, pain point)
- Open their CRM record in HubSpot
- Have ROI calculator ready with their numbers pre-filled
- Have demo presentation open (presentation/index.html)

## Call Structure

### 1. Opening & Rapport (0:00 - 3:00)
**Say:** "Hi [Name], thanks for booking this. Before we dive in — I saw you run a [industry] business with about [size] people. What made you curious about AI employees?"

**Listen for:** Their specific pain point. Don't pitch yet. Let them talk.

**If they're quiet:** "Most [industry] leaders I talk to are dealing with either support volume they can't scale, or leads that go cold because nobody responds fast enough. Which one hits closer to home?"

### 2. Discovery — Map Their Pain (3:00 - 12:00)
Ask these questions (in order):

1. "Walk me through what happens when a new lead comes in today. Who handles it? How fast?"
2. "What's the most repetitive task your team does every single day?"
3. "If you could wave a magic wand and automate one thing tomorrow, what would it be?"
4. "How many hours per week would you estimate your team spends on tasks that are essentially the same thing over and over?"
5. "What's that costing you? Not just in salary — in missed opportunities, slow responses, errors?"

**Take notes.** Use their exact words later in the pitch.

### 3. Scanner Results Review (12:00 - 17:00)
**Say:** "So based on what you told me plus the scan you ran, we identified [N] automation opportunities. Let me walk you through the top 3."

Show their scanner results. Connect each opportunity to what they just told you.

### 4. Live Demo — Show, Don't Tell (17:00 - 27:00)
**Say:** "Let me show you exactly what this looks like in practice."

**Open presentation/demo-fullscreen.html or the relevant demo tab on the website.**

Show the agent that matches their #1 pain:
- Support pain → Customer Success Agent demo
- Sales pain → Sales Agent demo  
- Admin/ops pain → Operations Agent demo
- Finance pain → Finance Agent demo

**After demo:** "That's running right now for 120+ businesses. Same thing, customized to your [CRM/helpdesk/tools]."

### 5. ROI Calculation (27:00 - 32:00)
**Open presentation/roi-live.html with their numbers.**

**Say:** "Let's do the math with your actual numbers."

Input their real figures. Show the savings.

**Key moment:** When the annual savings number appears, pause. Let them absorb it. Don't fill the silence.

**Say:** "So that's $[X] you're leaving on the table every year. The question isn't whether this makes sense — it's which agent to start with."

### 6. Recommendation (32:00 - 37:00)
**Say:** "Based on everything you've told me, here's what I'd recommend..."

Recommend 1-2 agents maximum for initial deployment. Explain why.

Show the relevant pricing tier. Frame it against their calculated savings.

**Say:** "We're talking about $[monthly cost] to save $[monthly savings]. That's a [X]x return."

### 7. Handle Objections (37:00 - 40:00)

**"It's too expensive"**
→ "I understand. Let me reframe: you're spending $[their annual waste] on manual work right now. Our monthly cost is less than what you lose in a single week of slow responses."

**"I need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What specifically do you want to think through? I want to make sure I've given you everything you need to make a confident decision."

**"We're not ready yet"**
→ "When would be the right time? Because the businesses that deploy now are building competitive advantage every day their competitors wait."

**"Can I try it first?"**
→ "The assessment call IS the trial — you've just seen exactly what it does. And we have a 30-day results guarantee. If you don't see ROI in 30 days, full refund."

**"I need to talk to my partner/team"**
→ "Absolutely. Would it help if I sent you a one-page summary with the ROI calculation so you have something concrete to share? I can also join a 15-minute call with them."

### 8. Close (40:00 - 43:00)
**Trial close:** "Based on what we've discussed, does the [Growth/Starter] plan make sense for where you are right now?"

**If yes:** "Great. I'll send you the signup link right now — takes 2 minutes. We'll schedule your onboarding call for this week."

**If maybe:** "Tell you what — our founding member pricing is locked for this month. If you sign up today, that rate is yours for life. After [date], it goes up to the regular price."

### 9. Next Steps (43:00 - 45:00)
**Say:** "Here's exactly what happens next:"
1. You click the link and choose your plan (2 minutes)
2. We schedule your onboarding call (this week)
3. You share tool access (takes 30 minutes)
4. We build and deploy your agent (14 days)
5. You see results within the first week

**End:** "Thanks for your time, [Name]. I'm excited to build this for you. You're going to wonder how you ran without it."

## Post-Call (within 1 hour)
- Update HubSpot with call notes and next action
- Send follow-up email with ROI summary and signup link
- If they didn't close: schedule follow-up for day +3
- Add them to the appropriate nurture sequence
